Breastfeeding

Breastfeeding benefits not only the children but also the mother. It is beneficial for the health of both. The baby should be fed only breast milk for at least 6 months after delivery. The best way to breastfeed is to provide comfort and convenience to both mother and children. The mother should not feel stressed while breastfeeding. Since it may take five to 40 minutes to breastfeed, choose a comfortable place for this.

If a woman is breastfeeding, she can eat all things controlled or in moderation. However, it is possible that by eating certain types of food and beverages from the mother, the stomach of the baby is disturbed. Due to these, the baby may have stomach pain or gas problems. Which he starts crying more and you don't even understand why this is happening. However, every mother and child is different, so there are no strict rules about what to include and not include in your diet. Nevertheless, below are some things that breastfeeding women should not eat. Coffee- If a woman drinks coffee, soda or tea, then a small amount of caffeine is found in the milk, which the children are not able to digest properly. Due to this, they have problems of irritability, pain and sleeplessness. If the mother wishes to drink coffee too much, then after feeding the baby, she can do so. Sour Fruits- Some elements found in citrus fruits and their juices spoil the stomach of the child. This can make them nervous, they may become irritable, milk may be reversed. To avoid this problem, papaya or mango can be eaten to meet the vitamin C deficiency from citrus fruits. Chocolate- Chocolate also contains caffeine just like coffee. However, the amount of caffeine in it is less than that of coffee. So stop eating it immediatel. Broccoli- If a woman takes broccoli in food, it can affect the child even by night. He may have nervousness, stomach ache. To deal with this problem, if you eat broccoli a little and cook it in steam, then it will not affect the children so much. Garlic- Even if a woman likes to eat garlic, it may not taste good to the child. Eating garlic can also cause its smell in mother's milk. This smell can come in milk even after two hours of eating garlic. It is possible that the child does not drink milk. Peanuts- If a woman is breastfeeding, be careful while eating peanuts. Because if the family medical history includes allergies then you have to stay away from allergic things like peanuts. It is possible that even if the mother eats peanuts, the child may be allergic to it. Try to detect allergies to rashes or other symptoms in the child. Maize - Children are often allergic to maize. But it is a little difficult to find out whether such a young child is allergic to maize or not. For this, after eating mother maize, check whether allergy related symptoms emerge in the child. Peppermint- Peppermint reduces the production of breast milk. Therefore, if a woman is breastfeeding, do not use mint.
