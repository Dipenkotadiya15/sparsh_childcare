Vaccination

Care Children hospital is one of the trusted vaccination Centre in Surat. Our immunization Centre is serving in Surat since many years.

Human body's immune system helps fight and protect against different types of pathogens that cause infection. But it can't fight with few pathogens which causes serious illness. This is because our immune system don't recognize them. This is where Vaccination is helpful. It is a way to teach our immune system recognize and eliminate such pathogens. This helps with faster response to any such pathogen in future and protect against illness.

Most vaccines are given by injection and contains 2 parts the first one is the antigen and second one is the adjuvant.

Vaccination can help growing baby and help stop the new pathogens
