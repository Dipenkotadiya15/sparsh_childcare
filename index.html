About Us

Sparsh children hospital and NICU

Sparsh children hospital and NICU is one of the most trusted and best children hospital in Surat. It is the 1st pediatric hospital in adajan pal area to have all facilities for your children & newborns at one place. Equipped with modern diagnostic facilities and well trained staff, we thrive to provide low cost yet efficient treatment to our patients. Facilities at our clinic are OPD, IPD, Vaccination center, PICU, Level 3 NICU, 24Hrs Pharmacy and Emergency services.

Sparsh children hospital and NICU is having the best pediatrician in Surat. which ensures good treatment of your child. We at Sparsh Hospital make sure that everyone have access to the best child care facilities at our both Adajan and Velanja clinics. Our child specialist hospital in Surat is established with a vision to provide quality care and treatments using advanced methodology and equipment.
